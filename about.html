About gamechanger inc. and me*

How it all started...

The story of my fictional company and me is not a story of inspiration.

I did what everyone else around me did: I went through school and found a job. As an engineer, I was adequate but never quite successful. Fearing my career coming to a dead end, I escaped before I could be fired.

Off to the Land of the Rising Sun, I became a teacher. It was a decent but cyclical job. Realities of daily life would catch up to me, and I escaped.

I then became a translator. Many things in life was beyond my control: pandemic, economy, corporate restructuring... I had to escape once again.

I got tired of running and founded gamechanger inc. as a way to realize change on my terms. Since then, my fictional company and its tireless team have helped many imaginary people realize their power over their own lives.

We want to help you on your journey of change.
